Helpful Answers

Transform your home with confidence.

What paint should I use?

Start with VibrAInt. Use the AI-powered colour tool to visualise the look and feel you want for your space, then bring that direction to Olympic Paints so the team can match it to the right colour, finish, durability and paint performance.

How do I choose paint colours?

Look at flooring, furniture, textiles, daylight and the emotional quality you want. The Colour Cafe lets you compare palettes beside real materials before you commit.

What is the Colour Cafe?

It is an immersive design destination where coffee, colour consultations, feel boards and expert guidance help you design your future home.

How do colour consultations work?

A consultant listens to your goals, studies your space and guides you through palettes, finishes and practical choices so you leave with a clear direction.
